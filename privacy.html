Privacy information

Last updated: June 2, 2026. This page describes the current closed-beta behavior of DUBI.

Data used by DUBI

DUBI uses the profile information you enter, nutrition preferences, progress entries and optional wearable data to generate and adapt your nutrition plan.

Purpose

Your data is used to provide the DUBI service. It is not sold. During the beta, DUBI does not share your data with insurers or advertising partners.

Wearables

If you connect a wearable provider, DUBI stores the normalized daily signals needed by the app, such as steps, active calories, sleep and HRV. Provider authorization is managed through the connected wearable service.

Delete your data

You can permanently delete your DUBI account and saved data from Settings using “Delete my account and data”.

Beta notice

DUBI is a wellness and nutrition planning beta. It is not a medical diagnosis service and does not replace professional medical advice.
